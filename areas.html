THE GAG

The GAG Stage is the main stage of Bushwig. The seat of big name queens & the best international & local drag talent. Situated in the main room of the venue and host to headliners and heavy hitters, this is where the joys of drag really come alive. From breezy daytime treats to dazzling night time spectacles with fantastic production, lighting and NON-STOP-SHOWS.  See you down the front

LOVE ON THE DOWNLOW

Is our out-door disco paradise. With world renowned international DJ talent amongst Brooklyn favorites. Right next to the ‘Dark Room Cinema’ & next to the food spot serving fine foods… So you can stuff your face with sausages whilst dickmatizing. We got you.

GET STUFFED

In the mood for food? Right next to the Love Down Low stage try our fare from local vendors delicious food, decadent drinkies, carefree play and sweet, sweet music… Get stuffed! High lights include; Horrorchata’s Taco Queen & Babes Trust Big Fat BBQ.

BUSHSWAG!

Some of the best vendors from across NYC & the states selling home-made wears, vintage, re-purposed clothing, trinkets, lighters, t-shirts, art, nails, wigs, shoes… everything.
The highlights: Free Nail Bar from our friends at SinfulColor’s, get your nail swag on for free with one of the world’s wildest nail polish & of course Official Bushwig 2017 shirts! Bring cash.
